Knowledge Exchange

I value sharing the knowledge of climate change with the general public, especially our next generation. I used to be a voluntary guide in the Harvard Natural History Museum for the climate change session with Teddy Amdur. Since 2020, I started a series of collaborations with Citizen Schools on providing public school students the opportunity to learn climate change.
