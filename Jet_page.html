Jet Dynamics



This study is my undergraduate project collaborating with Prof. Yang Zhang at Nanjing University.

Using zonal momentum budget, we quantified contributions of different dynamical processes to the interannual variability of the East Asian Jet and revealed a distinct up- and downstream controlling mechanism. Additionally, we revealed significant covariance between the East Asian Jet and Pacific Subtropical Ocean Front. The machenism is summarized in the figure below.
