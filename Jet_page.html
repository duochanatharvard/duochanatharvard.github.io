Jet Dynamics



I collaborate with Prof. Yang Zhang at Nanjing University.

Using the zonal momentum budget, we quantified contributions of different dynamical processes to the interannual variability of the East Asian Jet and revealed a distinct up- and downstream controlling mechanism.
